## Decision

NABCor is an evidence-aware AI Creative Operating System. Websites and other creative
assets are channel outputs of a shared brand-intelligence spine; NABCor is not defined
as a website builder, template system, or generator collection.

## Evidence

The product-owner instruction explicitly rejected the legacy repository definition and
requested a clean project matching the new vision. BC-001 additionally supports the
value of decisions, contracts, evidence discipline, and human gates, but one case does
not prove the whole category thesis.

## Consequences

The README, architecture, roadmap, and acceptance criteria begin with truth, direction,
evaluation, and memory. Channel implementation cannot redefine the product core.

## Revisit trigger

Repeated user research or experiments show that the cross-channel intelligence spine
does not create measurable value beyond a narrower product.
